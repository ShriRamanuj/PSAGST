GST Litigation Focus

Specialised Practice in GST Litigation

Pawan Soni & Associates focuses on GST litigation matters — from Show Cause Notices at the adjudicating authority level through First Appeals (FAA) and up to the GST Appellate Tribunal (GSTAT). The firm handles SCN replies, appeal drafting, personal hearing representation, and CESTAT matters for indirect tax disputes.

📄

SCN Reply — Show Cause Notice

Drafting of replies to GST Show Cause Notices — covering jurisdictional objections, without-prejudice pleadings, factual submissions, and legal grounds under the CGST Act 2017.

🏛️

First Appeal — FAA

Filing and representation of appeals before the First Appellate Authority (Joint Commissioner Appeals) — covering grounds of natural justice, jurisdiction, Section 75(7) penalty, and demand correctness.

⚖️

GSTAT — GST Appellate Tribunal

Preparation and filing of GSTAT appeals — Statement of Facts, Grounds of Appeal, Prayer clauses, and Interlocutory Stay applications under the CGST Act and GSTAT Procedure Rules 2025.

🔍

CESTAT Matters

Representation before the Customs, Excise and Service Tax Appellate Tribunal for legacy service tax and customs matters under the Finance Act 1994 and Customs Act 1962.

💡

GST Advisory & Classification

HSN classification disputes, refund matters for inverted duty structure, ITC eligibility issues, and advisory on complex GST transactions under Chapter 84, 55, and other schedules.

📊

Income Tax — SCN & Appeals

Replies to Income Tax notices and filing of appeals before the CIT(A) and ITAT under the Income Tax Act 1961, including Tax Audit related matters.

Pawan Soni & Associates is a Chartered Accountancy firm based in Ichalkaranji, Maharashtra, with a focus on GST litigation and indirect tax matters. The firm handles Show Cause Notice replies, first appeals before the Appellate Authority, and representations before the GST Appellate Tribunal (GSTAT).

The firm is located in Ichalkaranji, the textile manufacturing centre of Maharashtra, and serves businesses across the Kolhapur-Sangli region in their GST compliance and litigation requirements.

Income Tax advisory and appeals under the Income Tax Act 1961 are also handled by the firm, including Tax Audit matters and assessment proceedings.
